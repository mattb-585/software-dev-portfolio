Pet Tracker is an iOS application that has been one of my assignments for one of my college courses. My tasks included integrating user-friendly application design that meets the standards for iOS applications and programming the main user functionality for the application. My version of this application allows you add new ‘friends’ by filling out a form and allows you to add your friend’s pets on their profiles by also filling out a form.
